About

Hello!

I'm Daksitha Senel Withanage Don, a researcher specializing in generative AI technologies and their applications in interactive virtual agents. My work focuses on creating systems that understand and generate natural, human-like interactions to enhance digital communication.

With a passion for both technology and creative expression, I explore innovative approaches that blend research with art, striving to make virtual agents more engaging and intuitive.
