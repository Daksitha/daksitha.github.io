Abstract

In this demo paper, we introduce MeLaX, a real-time platform for integrating generative AI within interactive, photorealistic virtual agents. The system orchestrates speech recognition, natural language understanding, text-to-speech, and behavior generation in a unified pipeline to deliver context-sensitive and emotion-aware responses. With its no-code graphical user interface, MeLaX provides an accessible environment for researchers and developers to explore advanced human-agent interactions.
